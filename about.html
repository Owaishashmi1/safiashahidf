A decade ago, SS Foundation, a charitable and nonproﬁt organization embarked its journey starting from a small camp established at a densely populated area of Aisha Manzil, Karachi. SS's diligent efforts resulted in setting up camps which provide immediate aids to the urgent calls and matters.
